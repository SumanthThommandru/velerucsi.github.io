About Us


CSI Church,Veleru

The Church of South India (CSI) is one of the largest Protestant imagees in India, formed in 1947 by the union of several Christian denominations such as Anglican, Methodist, and Presbyterian. It is mainly spread across South India and is known for its unity, strong faith in the Bible, and worship centered on Jesus Christ. CSI is also actively involved in social service, running schools, hospitals, and community programs to help people in need.
